This project is done for a long-time client and a good friend from my high school days. The Client is opening a premium spa and nails shop located in Houston, Texas and needs a new logo and a business website that also allows for online booking and payment. The website should showcase different manicure and pedicure services, provide online appointments booking, and in-depth analytics to improve their business insights around the Houston metro area. The website also comes with original, thematic graphical assets to bolster the business’s professional credibility.

The website consists of 3 main components--overview, service details and online booking. We went with Squarespace as the builder platform due to its streamlined interface, developer-friendly tools and native online booking support.

I approach the branding project with a logo mark that best captures the feelings of serenity and elegance, and one that could be displayed in different formats throughout the storefront, you can read more about the logo design process here.

For the website, the goal was similar – create a clean, spacious website that has the premium look and feel to it while maintaining the elegant strokes of the logo mark. The Online Booking and Payment feature was added via a third-party plugin and integrated into the site so that it can be managed by the Client post-launch.
